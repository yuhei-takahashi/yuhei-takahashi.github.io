Yuhei Takahashi is currently pursuing a Ph.D. degree in the Department of Intelligent Information Engineering and Sciences, Doshisha University, Kyoto, Japan. His research interests include coding theory and radio communication systems.

He received the B.S. and M.S. degrees from the same Department, in 2021 and 2023, respectively. Since April 2023, he has been working as a communication systems engineer with Hitachi, Ltd., Japan.
